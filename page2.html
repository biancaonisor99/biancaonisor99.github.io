About me

I studied at Universitatea de Artă și Design in Cluj Napoca. There, I focused on painting and art therapy, but I also love graphic design, photography, sculpture and books. I like exploring various techniques and experimenting in different fields. I love both conceptual and classical art. I consider that changing perspective is helpful for improving ourselves.
I'm currently studying at Master's program of Digital media and game studies and working as a graphic designer in Cluj-Napoca.
The other passions that I have is literature, poetry and writing. I live and love aesthetic.
