Design process step by step

1

Communication

A good communication with the client is the first important step to a quality design service.
2

Research

The second step is the research the designer do based on the client preferences.
3

Brainstorming

Brainstorming is an important step for the designer to collect the ideas and choose the ones that fit with the client request.
4

Experiment

Experimenting with various concepts, design types, colors and shapes can bring to new ideas until the final product is coming up.
5

Simulation

The client receives a simulation of the ordered product/s and can accept or deny it. The communication is very important to solve any problems.
